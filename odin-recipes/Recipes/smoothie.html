Mango Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups mango chopped roughly
  • 1 and 1/2 cups milk boiled and cooled
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 nos almonds

Step by step to a make a Mango Smoothie

  1. To a clean dry mixer jar add 1 and 1/2 cups chopped mangoes. I used alphonso variety.
  2. Add 1 and 1/2 cups fresh milk, boiled and cooled.
  3. Add 1/2 cup thick curd.
  4. Add 2 tbsp honey. Increase to 3 tbsp if your mangoes are not that sweet.
  5. Add 2 nos almonds. You can soak in hot water and add to get it blended easily.
  6. Now all ready to blend.
  7. Blend until thick and creamy. Adjust consistency according to your liking. Thin it with water if you prefer slightly runny smoothie.
  8. Smoothies are slightly thick so adjust consistency according to your liking.
  9. Pour into serving glass.
  10. Garnish with a small sprig of mint leaves. Enjoy!
  11. Enjoy Mango Smoothie fresh!
