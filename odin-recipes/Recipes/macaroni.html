Macaroni

Macaroni is a dry pasta variety which is more like a curved tube. The curved tube variety is elbow macaroni which I have used here and ther other variety is a narrow tube. But the most common variety available here is the elbow macaroni

Ingredients
  • 1 cup of macaroni pasta
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp garlic finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp ginger finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup of onion finely chopped
  • 1 cup of tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup capsium
  • 2 tbsp carrots
  • 1 tbsp tomato sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 cup pasta cooked water
  • 1 tsp coriander leaves
  • salt to taste

Macaroni cooking steps
  1. Bring 4 cups water to boil in a large pot. Get ready with all other ingredients.
  2. When water starts to boil add 1 tsp oil along with salt. Let it boil.
  3. Add 1 heaped cup macaroni pasta variety.
  4. Give a quick mix.
  5. Cook until fork tender. It may take 5-6 mins depending upon the pasta variety and size. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta cooked water for later use.
  6. Drain water and Set aside.
  7. Rinse it with cold water to prevent further cooking.
  8. Heat oil in a pan and add 1 tsp garlic, 1/2 tsp ginger chopped finely. Saute for a minute until golden.
  9. Then add 1/2 cup onion finely chopped along with salt.
  10. Saute till transparent and golden.
  11. Add 1 cup tomatoes(2 medium sized), 1/4 cup capsicum, 2 tbsp carrots finely chopped.
  12. Saute till tomatoes turn mushy and raw smell leaves
  13. Cook covered for few mins or until the veggies turn slightly soft.
  14. Add 1 tsp red chilli powder, 1/2 tsp garam masala powder and 1 tsp mixed herbs.
  15. Give a quick saute
  16. Add 1 tbsp tomato sauce along with 1/2 cup pasta cooked water.
  17. Give a quick mix and let the sauce boil for a minute
  18. Finally add cooked pasta
  19. Toss well until the masalas are well coated.
  20. Add 1 tsp chopped coriander leaves. The pasta should not be dry.
  21. Give a quick mix and switch off.
